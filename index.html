Minor Dive into The Harmonic Expanse

Oh no Emma talks about her worldbuilding (again)

Overview

Major Subregions

The Lustrous Abyss

Located deep within the recesses of the Harmonic Expanse, The Lustrous Abyss is considered the epicenter when it comes to the region's creation.

Discordant Breach

Despite the sheer size of the Harmonic Expanse, entering into the region itself has been known to be surprisingly difficult. Such difficulty can be attributed to the massive subregion that lines (what is considered to be) the outer edges of the domain.

Mirage's Absence

Compared to the rest of Siphanthotoa's domain, Mirage's Absence is suspiciously void of life.

Notable Organizations

Church of the Singing Spheres

The primary religious body dedicated to the worship of Siphanthotoa, The Church of the Singing Spheres can be found all across the Harmonic Expanse, sometimes even testing their reach within the fringes of the Lustrous Abyss.

Black Space Trader's Consortium (Harmonic Expanse Branch)

The Black Space Trader's Consortium is already well-known to be one of the wealthiest trader's organizations to exist, and the Harmonic Expanse Branch is no exception to this rule.

Hadolo's Siphantite Assembly and Repair®

One of many workshops that specializes in working with devices powered by the Harmonic Expanse's most abundant material, Hadolo's Siphantite Assembly and Repair® and what they provide is often touted as being the best the market can offer. Whether or not these claims are true depends on the customer.
